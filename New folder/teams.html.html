This is a place where we as a group of individuals together to achieve their goal. Team is a group of people who are interdependent with respect to information, resources, knowledge and skills and who seek to combine their efforts to achieve a common goal. A team works as a whole together to achieve certain things. Teams can meet in-person ,directly face-to-face or virtually when practicing their values and activities or duties. A team's communication is significantly important to their relationship. Teams are in need of transformational leaders not more managers, with the important caveat that the world does not function well without managers. Idealized. transformational leaders engage in the following behaviors:
  1. Influence
  2. Inspirational Motivation
  3. Individualized Consideration
